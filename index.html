About Traffic Slight

Navigate Smarter, Ride Efficiently

Traffic Slight is a GPS-based mobile application developed to support motorcycle riders in Valenzuela City. It provides real-time traffic updates, optimized route suggestions, fuel usage estimates, and automated maintenance alerts.

The goal is to help riders avoid congestion, reduce fuel costs, and enhance riding efficiency through intelligent, data-driven navigation.

How Traffic Slight Works

1

Input Motorcycle Details

Users input motorcycle details such as fuel type, oil type, motorcycle age, and odometer reading.

2

Retrieve GPS Traffic Data

The app retrieves live GPS traffic data to analyze current road conditions in real-time.

3

Generate Optimized Routes

Traffic Slight generates optimized routes based on traffic conditions and fuel efficiency.

4

Real-Time Updates

As the rider travels, the system updates estimated fuel consumption and maintenance indicators.

5

Trip Summary & Insights

After each trip, the app provides a summary including distance traveled, fuel used, and maintenance alerts.
